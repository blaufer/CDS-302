All of the definitions below come from the United States Bureau of Labor Statistics (http://www.bls.gov/).

Physicist - Conduct research into physical phenomena, develop theories on the basis of observation and experiments, and devise methods to apply physical laws and theories.

Chemist - Conduct qualitative and quantitative chemical analyses or experiments in laboratories for quality or process control or to develop new products or knowledge.

Biologist - Biologists study organisms and try to understand how these organisms live, grow, and interact with their environments.

My definition for a Data Scientist is that they study complex data and develop techniques to analyze and study the datasets.
